WHEN TWO WORLDS COLLIDE

I would choose this title because it brings strong images, memories and feelings to the mind of a reader. It would bring the reader attention closely and ask what happens in the book when two worlds collide. This way the reader is already inquisitive before reading the book creating an urge to the reader to know what is happening. The title highlights Noah’s position between the white and black worlds of South Africa.

GROWING UP UNDER ARPATHEID

It describes how Trevor Noah’s life was shaped by racial segregation, the illegality of his birth between a black woman and a white man which was a crime at that time in South Africa. It shows how his birth was a violation of the law under the apartheid rule. Highlights the experience of apartheid that was represented with poverty among the blacks having Trevor been born by a black woman and the constant struggle to navigate a world where he is considered a crime in instances where he had no right of freedom as his movement was limited .
